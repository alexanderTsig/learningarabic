The CSC number (Card Security Code) on your credit card or debit card is a 3 digit number on VISA®, MasterCard® and Discover® branded credit and debit cards. On your American Express® branded credit or debit card it is a 4 digit numeric code.

Your CSC number can be located by looking on your credit or debit card, as shown in the image below.

Providing your CSC number to an online merchant proves that you actually have the physical credit or debit card - and helps to keep you safe while reducing fraud.

CSC numbers are NOT your card's secret PIN (Personal Identification Number). You should never enter your PIN number when asked to provide your CSC.

CSC numbers are also known as CVV numbers (Card Verication Value), as well as CVV2 numbers, which are the same as CVV numbers, except that they have been generated by a 2nd generation process that makes them harder to "guess".
